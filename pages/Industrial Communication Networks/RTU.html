Modbus RTU

Modbus RTU (Remote Terminal Unit) is a serial communication protocol commonly used over RS-485 or RS-232 networks. It uses binary data format, which makes communication efficient and compact.

Characteristics of Modbus RTU include:

  • Serial communication-based protocol.
  • Uses RS-485 for multi-drop networks.
  • Master-slave communication structure.
  • Suitable for short to medium distance communication.

Modbus RTU is widely used in field-level communication, connecting PLCs with sensors, meters, and remote devices.
